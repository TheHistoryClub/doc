A resolution to amend the Secretary of the club on record to Ethan Soo.

In The History Club

October 24th 2023

Mr.Lu (for Mr.Soo)  submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Club Meeting for a vote.

RESOLUTION

  1. Formally amend the club secretary of the club on record to Ethan Soo:
  1. On today’s meeting record, insert “Change the secretary of the club on record to Ethan Soo”.

  1. In accordance with the present club charter:
  1. The requirement for a 21 day notes is waived upon adoption of this resolution.
  2. This resolution requires a simple majority of the present members.
