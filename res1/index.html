C.Res.1

A resolution to amend the present club charter and set forth the general long-term plan of The History Club.

In The History Club

October 24th 2023

Mr.Lu (for himself, and Mr.Cho)  submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the Club Meeting for a vote.

RESOLUTION

  1. Formally amend the club charter to express the merge of the two history clubs:
  1. Amend the club charter to such described in attachment a of the resolution.

  1. Upon adoption of the resolution, the history club will formally initiate the charter process for the next application process:
  1. The History Club will be re-charted as a tier 2 club;
  2. The History Club will restate the club charter, amended or not,in its entirety, in the new application process.

  1. In accordance with the present club charter,
  1. The requirement for a 21 day notes is waived upon adoption of this resolution.
  2. This resolution requires a two-thirds majority vote.
